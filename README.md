# HR_ATTRITION
# HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes employee attrition patterns using Python and Power BI.  
The goal is to identify key factors influencing employee turnover and help HR teams improve retention strategies.

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Power BI
- Data Visualization
- Exploratory Data Analysis

## Dataset
The dataset contains employee information such as:
- Age
- Department
- Job Role
- Salary
- Job Satisfaction
- Work Life Balance
- Years at Company
- Attrition Status

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Cleaning (Python)
- Handle missing values
- Convert categorical variables
- Prepare dataset for analysis

### 2. Exploratory Data Analysis
Key analyses performed:
- Attrition distribution
- Attrition by department
- Attrition by job role
- Attrition vs salary
- Experience vs attrition

### 3. Dashboard Development (Power BI)

The dashboard consists of **three pages**:

### Page 1: Overview
Provides a high-level summary of workforce statistics.

KPIs:
- Total Employees
- Attrition Count
- Attrition Rate
- Average Age
- Average Salary
- Average Years at Company

Visuals:
- Attrition distribution
- Attrition by department
- Salary distribution
- Attrition by age group
- Attrition by years at company

---

### Page 2: Attrition Drivers
Analyzes the root causes of employee attrition.

KPIs:
- Attrition Rate
- Employees Who Left
- Average Years Before Leaving
- Average Salary (Attrition)

Visuals:
- Attrition by marital status
- Attrition distribution by job role
- Attrition by years at company
- Attrition by distance from home
- Job satisfaction heatmap
- Attrition by job level
- Decomposition tree (root cause analysis)

---

### Page 3: Employee Insights
Provides deeper workforce insights.

KPIs:
- Average Monthly Salary
- Average Total Experience
- Average Tenure
- Average Performance Score

Visuals:
- Average salary by job role
- Promotion gap analysis
- Work life balance by department
- Experience vs salary relationship
- Job involvement vs performance heatmap
- Work life balance vs job satisfaction heatmap

---

## Key Insights
- Employees with less than 5 years tenure have the highest attrition.
- Overtime significantly increases attrition risk.
- Some roles such as Sales Executive have higher turnover.
- Distance from home influences employee retention.
- Job satisfaction strongly correlates with attrition.

---

## Business Value
This dashboard helps HR teams:
- Identify departments with high attrition
- Understand employee satisfaction patterns
- Improve retention strategies
- Make data-driven workforce decisions
